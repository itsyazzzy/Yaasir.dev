## The Problem
Students in Somaliland lacked a centralized, free resource for accessing past exam papers and study materials, leading to unequal educational opportunities.

## The Solution
Built a scalable content delivery platform where students can easily search, view, and download educational resources. Implemented a robust admin dashboard for content management.
