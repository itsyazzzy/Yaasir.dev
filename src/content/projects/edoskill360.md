## The Problem
Traditional freelance platforms lack robust mechanisms for verifying skills and identity, leading to a trust deficit between employers and professionals.

## The Solution
Built a multi-role platform with a 'Trust Score' and an AI-powered verification pipeline for identity and skills, creating a secure ecosystem for freelancers and employers.
