## The Problem
Communities in drought-prone regions lack access to real-time, reliable information about water sources, hindering their ability to respond effectively to water scarcity.

## The Solution
Developed a community-powered platform that gathers real-time data via USSD and mobile reporting. The system uses community verification to ensure data accuracy, presenting it through live maps and dashboards to enable early action against drought.
