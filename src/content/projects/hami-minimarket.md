## The Problem
Small local businesses often struggle with complex e-commerce platforms and need a lightweight solution for listing products and managing basic orders.

## The Solution
Created a streamlined e-commerce app with product search/filtering, a client-side shopping cart using LocalStorage, and a simple checkout process.
